Bohnanza Duel

Get ready to go Bohnanza about beans!! In this game you grow beans! This is a 2 player version of the originally Bohnanza bean growing game. You race to grow beans and turn them into coins! The player with the most coins wins the game! Bring your A game to this quick and fun date night game!

  • Game Score: 9/10
  • Best Number of Players: 2
  • Average Play Time: 30 Minutes
